    Services

    Advancierge Africa Strategic Solutions Limited (AASSL) offers tailored, high-quality research and consultancy services to businesses, governments, NGOs, and development programs across Africa. Our expertise spans strategic planning, market and social research, feasibility studies, capacity building, project management, and health consultancy. We leverage innovative technology and evidence-based insights to deliver practical solutions that drive sustainable growth and competitive advantage. Partner with us to transform your vision into actionable strategies.

    STRATEGIC PLANS DEVELOPMENT AND REVIEWS

    AASSL is a global strategy consulting firm helping organizations drive growth through effective strategy execution. We foster open, honest leadership dialogue to challenge assumptions and align vision with action.

    RESEARCH & POLICY

    We leverage on our rigorous and analytical approach to applied research to generate insights that power our recommendations to our clients across the public, private, not-for-profit and international development sectors

    ORGANIZATION CAPACITY BUILDING

    AASSL empowers organizations through high-quality, tailored training and consultancy in management, research, and professional development. We strengthen institutional capacity across Africa with programs in SOPs, performance management, fundraising, data analysis, and more.

    FEASIBILITY STUDIES AND BUSINESS PLAN DEVELOPMENT

    AASSL helps businesses make informed decisions through in-depth feasibility studies and market research. We gather timely, relevant insights to support market entry, product development, brand strategy, and more.

    PROJECT MANAGEMENT

    AASSL delivers end-to-end project management solutions—from concept development to implementation, monitoring, and evaluation. We support organizations in managing complex projects across sectors like Livelihoods, WASH, and Climate Change Adaptation.

    GRANT AND FUND MANAGEMENT

    AASSL offers comprehensive grant and fund management services, supporting clients from concept note and proposal development to donor reporting and compliance. We ensure efficient fund utilization, transparent financial tracking, and alignment with donor requirements.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Get answers to common questions about our research and consultancy services across Africa

    What types of research methodologies do you use?

    We employ both qualitative (Focus Groups Discussions, In-depth Interviews, Ethnographies) and quantitative (Face-to-face interviews, Telephone surveys, Online surveys) methodologies. Our approach is tailored to each project's specific needs, using advanced tools like ODK applications on tablet computers for accurate data collection.

    Which African markets do you cover?

    We are headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya with an office in Kisumu, and work across multiple African countries including Uganda, Somalia, Tanzania, and Rwanda. We collaborate with trusted local partners to ensure culturally relevant insights in each market.

    How do you ensure data quality?

    We adhere to international research standards with strict quality assurance protocols. All projects receive 100% supervision from our experienced field managers, and we use technology with built-in control functions to minimize errors. Our analytical team adds strategic value through expert interpretation of findings.

    What industries do you specialize in?

    We serve diverse sectors including government agencies, NGOs, private businesses, and donor-funded programs. Our expertise spans strategic planning, market research, health consultancy, organizational capacity building, and project management across multiple industries.

    How long does a typical strategic planning engagement take?

    The duration varies based on organizational size and complexity, but most strategic planning engagements take 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to final delivery. This includes stakeholder interviews, workshops, plan development, and implementation roadmaps. We prioritize creating actionable plans that can be implemented immediately.

    Can you help with both strategy development and implementation?

    Yes, we offer end-to-end services from strategy formulation through execution. Our approach includes translating strategies into operational objectives, defining metrics, prioritizing projects, and aligning leadership teams to ensure successful implementation across all organizational levels.
